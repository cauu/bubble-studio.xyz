## Cardano Daily Digest: Ecosystem Updates at Your Fingertips

VODA's core mission is to be the information aggregation platform for the Cardano community. We've now taken a major step forward: **automated daily Cardano ecosystem digests**.

Every day, the system automatically crawls tweets from 200+ Cardano ecosystem-related Twitter accounts, then uses AI to filter, score, and cluster-analyze the content into a structured daily report. Each digest covers five dimensions:

- **Key Highlights**: The 2-3 most important ecosystem developments
- **Governance Discussions**: DRep, SPO, and community discussions on proposals
- **Community Voices**: Perspectives and feedback from the global community
- **Technical Updates**: Protocol upgrades and development progress
- **Project News**: Latest developments from ecosystem projects

Every item includes links to the original tweets, so readers can trace back and verify the sources.
